Club Description

This club intents to represent the whole community at an equal level. Not only the famous investors are important for BCH to succeed, but any single member of the community is part of the success.

You can take an active part in this club or you can create a similar club in your city and we will support you. Any member is vital for the community to exist and we will need many people to win this battle against mainstream propaganda and other human failures.

Anyone in the community can possibly earn an active income from trading cash for coins, so you can feed your kids and dogs even when the dollar and euro crashes. Bitcoins can not be closed down, like any bank account can be closed down.

We see it as a basic human right to secure our survival in these times of global economic warfare.

This club will need active members, which will be actually trading in the club, to create this amazing meeting spot for the community.

You are welcome to be part of this Ghandi Bitcoin Movement, we want to resist peacefully against censorship, we want to resist peacefully against the haters, so that in the end we have a better world with more freedom and love.
